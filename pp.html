1. Who We Are

Kidolens is an AR-based educational platform developed by Kidolens Private Limited we with the goal of making learning fun and interactive for children through technology.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum information necessary to operate and improve our Services. This may include:

a. Information You Provide:

  • Parent/guardian name and email (during registration)
  • Optional profile info (e.g., child’s nickname, age group)
  • Support inquiries and feedback

b. Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information (type, OS, browser)
  • IP address
  • App usage data (e.g., time spent, features accessed)
  • Crash reports or error logs

We do not collect personally identifiable information from children without verified parental consent.

3. How We Use the Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and personalize the Kidolens experience
  • Improve educational content and user interaction
  • Communicate with parents/guardians (e.g., updates, feedback)
  • Ensure the safety, security, and functionality of the Services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Children's Privacy

Kidolens complies with:

  • COPPA – Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (U.S.)
  • GDPR-K – General Data Protection Regulation for Kids (EU)

We do not knowingly collect or store personal data from children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent) without verifiable parental consent.

Parents may review, update, or request deletion of their child's data at any time by contacting us at support@kidolens.com.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers (e.g., for hosting, analytics, customer support)
  • Legal authorities if required by law
  • In the event of a business transfer (e.g., acquisition or merger)

6. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard practices to secure your data, including:

  • SSL encryption
  • Access control and authentication
  • Secure infrastructure

While we strive to protect your data, no system is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights and Choices

As a parent or guardian, you may:

  • Access, update, or correct your child’s information
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Request deletion of your or your child’s data

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@kidolens.com.

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use limited cookies or device identifiers to:

  • Store preferences
  • Enable AR content functionality
  • Analyze app performance

We do not use cookies for targeted advertising on children's accounts.

9. Third-Party Links

Kidolens may include links to third-party educational resources. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external websites.

10. International Users

If you are using Kidolens outside India, your data may be processed in India or other countries that may not have the same data protection laws as your jurisdiction.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If significant changes are made, we will notify users via email or an in-app message. Please review this policy periodically.
