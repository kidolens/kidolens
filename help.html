Help Center

Find answers to common questions or get in touch with our support team.

What is Kidolens?

Kidolens is an augmented reality (AR) learning app that makes education engaging and interactive for kids by combining curriculum content with immersive 3D experiences.

Is Kidolens safe for children?

Yes, Kidolens is designed with safety and privacy in mind. We comply with COPPA and GDPR-K, and parental supervision is recommended while using AR features.

Do I need an internet connection to use Kidolens?

Some AR content may require an internet connection to load or update. However, basic features may work offline after initial setup.

How do I report a bug or issue?

If you encounter a bug or technical problem, please contact us at support@kidolens.com with a description and screenshot, if possible.

How do I delete my account or my child’s data?

You can request account or data deletion by emailing us at support@kidolens.com. We’ll process your request within 7 working days.

Is there a subscription fee?

Kidolens offers free and premium content. You can enjoy basic lessons at no cost, but full access requires a subscription. Check the pricing inside the app for current plans.
